About Me

My name is Damon Chau and I am currently a Systems Design Engineering student at University of Waterloo.

I was born in Toronto but I currently reside in the City of Maple. I attended St. Robert Catholic High School in Thornhill and received an IB Certificate for Standard Level Math and Standard Level French. Later, I decided to drop out as the time constraints and schedule for IB did not allow for any electives that I wanted to participate in, namely computer science classes. My love for coding started here. My teacher for computer science sparked my interest in the discipline. He taught me the fundamentals of programming and Python. He inspired me to conduct self-learning of other languages. To date, I have learned Python in his class, taught myself HTML5 and CSS3, JavaScript while C++ is being taught in my university course, SYDE 121.

As a Systems Design Engineering student, I learn and integrate knowledge from multiple disciplines of engineering into a functional whole. I chose Systems Design Engineering because it offers me the chance to explore engineering as a whole instead of learning about just one discipline.
