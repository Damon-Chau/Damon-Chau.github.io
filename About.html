My name is Damon Chau and I am in Systems Design Engineering. If you don't know what that is, go read up on it here The too long; didn't read version of this is:

  • multi-disciplinary engineering
  • integrating parts from multiple disciplines of engineering into a functional whole
  • consideration of human interaction throughout the whole design process
I like to think of it as LEGO blocks where each LEGO block represents the products of different streams of engineering and the person putting it all together to make some sort of design.
