About me

My name is Damon Chau and I am a Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo.

I was born in Toronto and currently reside in the City of Maple. I attended St. Robert Catholic High School where I was an honour roll student for all 4 years of high school. In my grade 12 year, I started taking Computer Science Class. The teacher that taught this course inspired me to pursue a STEM related field. He taught me the fundamentals of programming and the software development life cycle and continuously pushed me to learn outside of class.
